\subsection{Fundamental Theorem of Calculus}
 
    The Fundamental Theorem of Calculus states that there exists a function G(x)
    defined by
    \[
    G(x) = \int_a^x g(t)\,dt
    \]
    such that
    \[
    \frac{d}{dx}G(x) = g(x)
    \]
    the function G(x) is an antiderivative of g(x)
